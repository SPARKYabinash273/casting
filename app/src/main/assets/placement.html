Placement Cell

An Independent placement office is adding sapphires to the crown of JEPPIAAR SRR Engineering College, Chennai making the sprout of the college more sanguine. The cell organizes campus placements for the students and they are properly guided and trained on the necessities keeping in place with the state of art today's technology by placement officer.
Full Width Slider

Pre Placement talks motivation classes, Aptitude test, Group Discussions and Mock Interviews are being conducted frequently. Aptitude tests, Technical Tests are conducted twice a week for all year students, which made the students to strive against the present competing world.

An important role is satiated by the placement cell in counseling and guiding the students for the successful career placements. The placement cell keeps close liaison with various industrial establishments and industries. The placement officer ensures and takes care to provide the best arrangements and hospitality for the visiting executives.

Every year, more than 27 companies recruit students from our Institute, Some of the recruiters are:
WIPRO TECHNOLOGIES, COMPUTER SCIENCES CORPORATION, INFOSYS, VIRTUSA, TECH MAHINDRA, HEXAWARE TECHNOLOGIES, NTT DATA, SCOPE INTERNATIONAL, OFS, SENSIPLE, ZYLOG SYSTEMS, R-SYSTEMS, ADAPTAVANT TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS, SANMINA, SANMAR, RR DONNELLEY, COVENANT, ROYAL BANK OF SCOTLAND ETC.,
